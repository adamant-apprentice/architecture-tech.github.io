About me

As a newly graduated Architectural Technician based in sunny California, I’m passionate about turning design concepts into precise, functional plans. With a keen eye for detail and a love for drafting, I thrive on bringing ideas to life through meticulous technical drawings and innovative problem-solving. When I’m not working on the next big project, you can find me catching waves along the California coast, where my love for surfing fuels my creative inspiration. Balancing technical precision with a laid-back surfer’s mindset, I aim to contribute to projects that shape beautiful, sustainable spaces.
